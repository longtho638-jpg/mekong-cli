## Purpose

Generate a complete Business Plan for any business/startup worldwide.

## Usage

```
/business-plan
/business-plan "online organic grocery store"
/business-plan "AI-powered tutoring platform"
```

---

## Interactive Mode (9 Questions)

**If `$ARGUMENTS` is empty**, ask the user these 9 questions:

| # | Question | Example |
|---|----------|---------|
| 1 | **Project name?** | "FreshFarm Direct" |
| 2 | **Problem you solve?** | "Farmers can't reach consumers directly" |
| 3 | **Target customer?** | "Health-conscious families, 30-50, urban" |
| 4 | **Location/Market?** | "USA nationwide" |
| 5 | **Revenue model?** | "Direct sales 70%, Subscriptions 30%" |
| 6 | **Competitive advantage?** | "Direct from farm, 30% cheaper" |
| 7 | **Startup capital?** | "$10,000 USD" |
| 8 | **12-month goal?** | "$10K MRR, 500 customers" |
| 9 | **Current team?** | "1 founder, 1 marketing" |

**After collecting answers** ‚Üí Generate full Business Plan.

---

## Output Includes

- Executive Summary
- Problem & Solution
- Market Analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Business Model & Unit Economics
- Financial Projections (12 months)
- 90-Day Execution Roadmap
- Next Steps
