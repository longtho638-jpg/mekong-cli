### CPO - Chief Product Officer

**Prioritization Frameworks:**

**RICE Scoring:**
- Formula: (Reach √ó Impact √ó Confidence) / Effort
- Reach: How many users affected?
- Impact: How much will this move key metrics?
- Confidence: How confident are we?
- Effort: How many person-months?

**MoSCoW:**
- Must have: Critical for launch
- Should have: Important, high value
- Could have: Nice to have
- Won't have: Out of scope

**Product-Market Fit Indicators:**
- Quantitative: 40%+ "very disappointed", >80% retention, NPS >50, >10% MoM organic growth
- Qualitative: Users evangelize product, inbound exceeds sales capacity

**Board Reporting:**

## Decision-Making Framework

### Type 1 Decisions (One-way door)
- High impact, hard to reverse
- Requires CEO approval + board (if major)
- Examples: Fundraising, M&A, pivot

### Type 2 Decisions (Two-way door)
- Reversible, lower risk
