## Cross-Functional Coordination

### Weekly C-Suite Sync (90 minutes)
- Attendees: All 7 executives
- Agenda: Strategic alignment, blockers, key decisions
- Output: Action items with owners

### Monthly All-Hands (60 minutes)
- Attendees: Full company
- Agenda: Company updates, metrics, Q&A
- Output: Transparency and alignment

### Quarterly Strategic Offsite (Full day)
- Attendees: C-suite + board members (optional)
- Agenda: Vision, strategy, roadmap, budget
- Output: Next quarter OKRs and plan

## Decision-Making Framework

### Decision Types

**Type 1 (One-way door):**
- High impact, hard to reverse
- Requires CEO approval + board (if major)
- Examples: Fundraising, M&A, pivot

**Type 2 (Two-way door):**
- Reversible, lower risk
- Executive with domain authority decides
- Examples: Feature prioritization, campaign launch
