# Viral Hook Generator

**Objective:** Generate high-converting hooks for content.
**Agent:** `marketing-wizard`

## 1. Input
- **Niche:** [e.g., Real Estate Agents]
- **Pain Point:** [e.g., No leads]
- **Solution:** [e.g., FB Ads]

## 2. Hook Formulas

### The "Negative" Hook
*Formula: "Stop doing [X] if you want [Y]."*
- **Draft:** Stop buying leads if you want to close more deals.

### The "Specific Number" Hook
*Formula: "How I got [Number] results in [Timeframe] without [Pain Point]."*
- **Draft:** How I got 50 listings in 30 days without cold calling.

### The "Secret" Hook
*Formula: "The [Adjective] secret that [Authority] doesn't want you to know."*
- **Draft:** The simple ad secret Zillow doesn't want you to know.

### The "List" Hook
*Formula: "[Number] tools/tips to [Benefit] (Number [X] is the best)."*
- **Draft:** 5 tools to automate your follow-up (Number 3 is free).

## 3. Virality Check (Score 0-10)
- **Curiosity:** Does it make them click "See More"?
- **Benefit:** Is the promise clear?
- **Emotion:** Does it trigger Fear/Greed/Joy?
