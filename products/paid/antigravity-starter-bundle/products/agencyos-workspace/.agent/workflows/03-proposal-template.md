# Proposal Template

**Objective:** Create a winning proposal.
**Agent:** `sales-closer`

## 1. Executive Summary
*Focus on the client's problem and the desired outcome.*
- **Problem:** [Client is losing X because of Y]
- **Solution:** [We will implement Z to fix Y]
- **Outcome:** [Client will gain X]

## 2. Scope of Work (The "What")
- **Phase 1:** Discovery & Strategy
- **Phase 2:** Design/Development
- **Phase 3:** Launch & Optimize

## 3. Timeline (The "When")
- **Kickoff:** Week 1
- **First Draft:** Week 3
- **Launch:** Week 6

## 4. Investment (The "How Much")
*Always offer options to anchor the price.*

### Option 1: Essential
- Core deliverables only.
- **Investment:** $[Amount]

### Option 2: Growth (Recommended)
- Core + High-value add-ons.
- **Investment:** $[Amount]

### Option 3: Dominance
- Full service + Ongoing support.
- **Investment:** $[Amount]

## 5. Next Steps
1. Choose an option.
2. Sign the contract (Link).
3. Pay deposit ($[Amount]).
4. Kickoff call on [Date].
