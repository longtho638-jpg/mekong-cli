## Writing Principles

1. **Clarity First** - Simple words, short sentences
2. **Value-Led** - Lead with benefits, not features
3. **Audience-Aware** - Write for the reader's level
4. **Action-Oriented** - Every piece should drive action
5. **SEO-Conscious** - Natural keyword integration
