## ðŸ“ Appendix: Common Questions

### Q1: Can I use crypto for payments?
**A:** Yes, but convert to fiat before withdrawing to VN bank. Tax office treats crypto as foreign currency. Document exchange rate at conversion time.

### Q2: What if my client pays via Upwork/Fiverr?
**A:** Still counts as foreign income if end client is foreign. Upwork/Fiverr acts as intermediary. Keep:
- Upwork/Fiverr payment receipts
- Client profile showing foreign location
- Withdrawal records to your bank

### Q3: Do I need Vietnamese invoices for foreign clients?
**A:** Not required by foreign clients, but YOU need them for tax filing. Issue Vietnamese e-invoices for your records, send commercial invoice to client.

### Q4: Can I deduct expenses as Individual Business?
**A:** Vietnam Individual Business tax (2% method) is on REVENUE, not profit. No expense deductions. That's why 2% flat is actually generous.

### Q5: What if I have both Vietnam and foreign clients?
**A:** Split revenue:
- Foreign clients: 0% VAT + 2% PIT
- Vietnam clients: 5% VAT + 2% PIT
- Keep separate records for each category

### Q6: When to hire an accountant?
**A:**
- Individual Business: Optional, can DIY if organized
- Company: MANDATORY, complexity too high

Cost-benefit: If revenue >100M VND/month, accountant pays for themselves in time saved + error prevention.
