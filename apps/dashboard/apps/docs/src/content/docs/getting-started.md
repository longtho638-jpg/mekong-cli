# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to AgencyOS! This guide will help you set up your agency workspace and start managing your business efficiently.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:
- An active AgencyOS account
- Basic details about your agency (name, size, services)
- Payment method for billing

## Step 1: Complete Your Profile

Navigate to **Settings > Profile** to update your personal information. Adding a profile picture and accurate role description helps your team identify you.

## Step 2: Configure Workspace

1. Go to **Settings > Workspace**
2. Upload your agency logo
3. Set your primary time zone and currency
4. Invite team members via email

## Step 3: Connect Payment Gateways

To start accepting payments:
1. Navigate to **Revenue > Settings**
2. Choose your provider (Stripe, PayPal, or SePay)
3. Enter your API keys
4. Enable test mode to verify the connection

## Step 4: Create Your First Service

Define what you sell:
1. Go to **CRM > Services**
2. Click **New Service**
3. Enter name, description, and pricing model (One-time or Recurring)
