The Founder Journey

Every stage. Every command. One terminal.

STAGE 1 — IDEA

Validate your idea before writing a single line of code.

/founder validate --product "AI coding assistant for solo devs"
/founder brand --name "DevMate" --audience "indie hackers"

STAGE 2 — BUILD

Ship fast. Plan, execute, verify — with automatic rollback.

mekong cook "Add Stripe billing to my FastAPI app"
mekong fix "Auth tokens expire after 10 minutes"

STAGE 3 — FUND

Raise from VCs. Detect red flags. Model your cap table.

/founder pitch --investor yc --rounds 10
/founder vc/term-sheet --file term.pdf
/founder vc/cap-table --raise 2m --pre-money 8m

STAGE 4 — SCALE

Hire agents. Track metrics. Run weekly CEO rhythm.

/founder hire --role "Head of Growth"
/founder metrics --period Q1 --anomaly-detect
/founder grow --channel "product-led"

STAGE 5 — IPO

From S-1 draft to bell ceremony. Public company ops included.

/founder ipo/pre-ipo-prep --audit
/founder ipo/s1 --exchange nasdaq
/founder ipo/ipo-day --exchange nasdaq

How It Works

Three steps. Zero babysitting.

01 — INSTALL

Get running in 60 seconds

pip install mekong-cli

02 — CONFIGURE

Connect any LLM provider

export LLM_BASE_URL="..."
export LLM_API_KEY="sk-..."

03 — RUN

Describe your goal, ship it

mekong cook "Build a SaaS"
