## Key Assumptions

| Assumption | Value | Sensitivity |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Monthly traffic growth | 25%/mo (Y1), 15%/mo (Y2) | High impact |
| Trial → paid conversion | 15% | High impact |
| Monthly churn (blended) | 6% | Medium impact |
| ARPU growth (tier mix) | $65 → $95 → $120 | Medium impact |
| LLM cost per MCU | $0.05 → $0.03 (volume) | Low impact |

---

## Funding Scenarios

### Scenario A: Bootstrapped (Preferred)
- No dilution
- Profitable by Q3 2026 (personal basis)
- Hire from revenue
- Slower growth, full control

### Scenario B: Pre-seed ($200K at $2M cap)
- 10% dilution
- Hire DevRel + engineer immediately
- Accelerates to $10K MRR by Jul 2026
- Required if organic growth stalls

### Scenario C: YC / Accelerator
- $500K for 7%
- Network effect + credibility
- Apply for W2027 batch if Y1 metrics strong
