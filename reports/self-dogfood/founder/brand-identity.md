# Brand Identity — Mekong CLI

**The Mekong River Metaphor** | Updated: March 2026

---

## The Core Metaphor

The Mekong River is 4,900km long. It originates in Tibet, flows through China, Myanmar, Laos, Thailand, Cambodia, and Vietnam before reaching the sea. It crosses every kind of terrain — mountains, jungles, deltas — without stopping.

**Mekong CLI is the same:** one system that flows through every layer of your business — founder strategy, marketing, engineering, ops — without switching tools.

The river doesn't ask permission. It finds the path. It delivers.

---

## Brand Pillars

### 1. Flow State
Work should flow like water. No context switching between 12 AI tools. One command, continuous execution. The PEV loop (Plan→Execute→Verify) IS flow state encoded in software.

**Expression:** "Just type it. It flows."

### 2. Strategic Depth (Binh Phap)
孫子兵法 isn't about aggression — it's about winning before the battle. Plan first, execute second, verify always. The brand carries intellectual weight without academic pretension.

**Expression:** "Plan. Execute. Verify. Win."

### 3. Builder's Honesty
No corporate polish. No "AI-powered synergy." Direct, technical, honest about what works and what doesn't. The self-dogfood reports ARE the product — real analysis, real numbers, real gaps.

**Expression:** "Built by someone who needed it."

### 4. Sovereignty
Any LLM. Your infrastructure. Your data. No vendor lock-in. The universal adapter architecture is a brand statement: *you own your AI stack*.

**Expression:** "3 env vars. Any LLM. You own it."

## Mekong River Visual Language

- **Water imagery:** Flow, current, delta (branching commands), source (LLM origin)
- **Geographic:** Maps, terrain layers, crossing borders = crossing business layers
- **Terminal aesthetic:** Dark water at night, cyan bioluminescence = our color palette
- **Scale:** A river that crosses 6 nations = a CLI that covers all 5 business layers

## Anti-Brand
