# Customer Discovery — Mekong CLI

**Goal:** Validate who needs an AI business CLI and why | **Target:** 20 interviews by May 2026

---

## Hypothesis

**Primary:** Solo founders and indie hackers spend 40%+ of their time on non-coding business tasks (writing, planning, ops) that AI could automate via CLI.

**Secondary:** Dev-tool power users (Cursor, Copilot users) want workflow automation beyond code completion.

## Interview Questions

### Screener (2 min)
1. Do you run or co-run a software product or agency?
2. How many people on your team?
3. Do you use AI tools (Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT) in your daily work?

### Problem Discovery (15 min)

**On current workflow:**
1. Walk me through your typical Monday morning. What's the first non-coding task you do?
2. What's the most tedious recurring task you do every week?
3. How much time do you spend on business tasks vs. actual building?

**On AI tools:**
4. Which AI tools do you use today? What do you use them for?
5. What's the most frustrating thing about your current AI tools?
6. Have you tried automating any business workflows with AI? What happened?

**On CLI specifically:**
7. Are you comfortable using a terminal? Rate 1–5.
8. If a CLI could run `mekong cook "write a sales email sequence"` and return a usable result — would you use it? What would make you trust it?
9. What would have to be true for you to pay $49/month for this?

**On pain:**
10. What would you do with 5 extra hours per week freed from ops tasks?

---

## What to Listen For

**Strong signal (build for this person):**
- "I spend hours writing proposals / specs / plans every week"
- "I use Claude but have to copy-paste everything manually"
- "I wish I could script my business like I script my code"
- "I'd pay for this if it saved me one client meeting"

**Weak signal (not your customer):**
- "I have a team that does this"
- "I don't use terminal, prefer GUI"
- "AI output always needs too much editing"
