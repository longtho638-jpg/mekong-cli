# VC Landscape — AI Dev Tools

**Purpose:** Map funding landscape for potential raise | **Updated:** March 2026

---

## Market Context

AI dev tools is the hottest VC category in 2025–2026. $4B+ invested in Cursor, Cognition (Devin), Anysphere, Magic, etc. But most funding goes to IDE/code-gen plays. Mekong CLI sits in a distinct niche: **AI-operated business workflows**, not just code completion.
