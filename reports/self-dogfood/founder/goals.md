## Goal Breakdown

### $10K MRR Path

| Tier | Price | Users Needed | MRR |
|------|-------|-------------|-----|
| Starter | $49 | 100 | $4,900 |
| Pro | $149 | 25 | $3,725 |
| Enterprise | $499 | 3 | $1,497 |
| **Total** | | **128** | **$10,122** |

Realistic mix: 80% Starter, 17% Pro, 3% Enterprise. Needs ~500 trial users to convert 128.

### 100 Stars Path

- Launch day: target 30 stars (PH + HN)
- Week 2-4: 20 more via Dev.to articles
- Month 2: 30 more via Reddit + Twitter organic
- Month 3: 20 more via word of mouth + contributor activity

### 50 WAU Path

- WAU = users running at least 1 `mekong` command per week
- Track via anonymous telemetry (opt-in) or Polar.sh active subscriptions
- Month 1: 10 WAU (early adopters)
- Month 2: 25 WAU (post-launch growth)
- Month 3: 50 WAU (retention + new users)
