# Brand Guide — Mekong CLI

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** March 2026 | **Status:** Draft

---

## Brand Essence

**Name:** Mekong CLI
**Tagline:** *"I am OpenClaw. I run this company."*
**Category:** AI-operated business platform
**Personality:** Strategic, direct, battle-tested, philosophically grounded

The Mekong River — longest in Southeast Asia, crossing 6 nations — represents unstoppable flow, adaptability across terrain, and the power of continuous motion. OpenClaw is the intelligence that navigates it.

## Logo Concepts

**Concept A: River + Terminal**
- Mekong river wave as underline to "M"
- Terminal cursor blink element
- Dark background, cyan gradient text

**Concept B: Claw Mark**
- Three diagonal slashes (OpenClaw mark)
- Overlaid on hexagonal grid (nodes/agents)
- Monochrome for versatility

**Concept C: Wordmark Only**
```
MEKONG CLI
━━━━━━━━━━
```
Clean, technical, no-nonsense. Preferred for README badges.

## Binh Phap Philosophy in Brand Voice

The brand draws from 孫子兵法 (Art of War) — not as aggression but as strategic clarity.

| Brand Principle | Binh Phap Source | Expression |
|----------------|-----------------|------------|
| Know your terrain | 地形 | "Audit before you build" |
| Win before fighting | 謀攻 | "Plan mode first, always" |
| Speed wins | 兵勢 | "One command ships everything" |
| No waste | 始計 | "0 tech debt, 0 bloat" |

**Voice attributes:** Direct, confident, zero fluff. Speaks to builders, not executives.

---

## Brand Don'ts

- No corporate blue or enterprise gray palettes
- No stock photos of happy people in offices
- No passive voice ("results may vary")
- No vague AI claims ("powered by AI")
