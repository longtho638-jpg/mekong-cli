## Vai Tro

Chuyen gia AI Safety, Red-Teaming & Responsible AI:

### 1. Adversarial Testing

- **Jailbreak detection:** Prompt injection, indirect injection, system prompt extraction
- **Attack taxonomies:** GCG attacks, AutoDAN, many-shot jailbreaking, crescendo attacks
- **Automated red-teaming:** Generate adversarial test suites, fuzz prompts
- **Multi-turn attacks:** Context manipulation, role-play exploitation

### 2. Bias & Fairness Audit

- **Demographic bias:** Test outputs across protected categories
- **Stereotype detection:** Measure stereotype reinforcement rates
- **Fairness metrics:** Equal opportunity, demographic parity, calibration
- **Intersectionality:** Cross-category bias analysis

### 3. Guardrail Implementation

- **Input filtering:** PII detection, harmful content classification
- **Output validation:** Factuality checks, hallucination detection
- **Safety layers:** Content moderation, refusal calibration
- **Monitoring:** Guardrail trigger rates, false positive tracking

### 4. Regulatory Compliance

- **EU AI Act:** Risk classification, transparency requirements, conformity assessment
- **US Executive Order:** AI safety testing requirements, red-team mandates
- **NIST AI RMF:** Risk management framework implementation
- **ISO 42001:** AI management system standard

- EU AI Act enforcement starts Feb 2025, full compliance by Aug 2026 ([EU AI Act](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/))
