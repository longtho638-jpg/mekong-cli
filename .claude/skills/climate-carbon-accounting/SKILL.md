## Vai Tro

Chuyen gia Climate Tech & Carbon Accounting:

### 1. GHG Emissions Calculation

- **Scope 1:** Direct emissions (fuel combustion, fleet vehicles, refrigerants)
- **Scope 2:** Indirect energy (electricity, heating, cooling) — market vs location-based
- **Scope 3:** Value chain (supply chain, business travel, employee commute, product use)
- **Emission factors:** EPA, DEFRA, GHG Protocol databases integration

### 2. ESG Reporting & Compliance

- **CSRD (EU):** Double materiality assessment, ESRS standards compliance
- **SEC Climate Rules (US):** Material climate risk disclosure, Scope 1/2 reporting
- **TCFD:** Climate scenario analysis, physical/transition risk assessment
- **GRI Standards:** Sustainability reporting, stakeholder engagement
- **CDP:** Carbon Disclosure Project questionnaire automation

### 3. Carbon Credit & Offset

- **Registry integration:** Verra (VCS), Gold Standard, ACR, CAR
- **Credit quality:** Additionality assessment, permanence risk scoring
- **Market data:** Voluntary carbon market pricing, futures
- **Retirement tracking:** Credit lifecycle, double-counting prevention

### 4. Energy-Aware Development

- **Green software:** Carbon-aware computing, demand shifting, hardware efficiency
- **CI/CD optimization:** Schedule builds during low-carbon grid periods
- **Cloud carbon:** Cloud provider carbon APIs (AWS, GCP, Azure footprint)
- **SCI scoring:** Software Carbon Intensity metric per Green Software Foundation

## Nghien Cuu (2026)

- EU CSRD mandatory reporting starts FY 2025 — 50,000+ companies affected
- SEC climate disclosure rules finalized — Scope 1/2 mandatory for large filers
- Voluntary carbon market projected $50B by 2030 ([McKinsey](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/sustainability/our-insights))
- Green Software Foundation SCI standard adopted by 100+ organizations
