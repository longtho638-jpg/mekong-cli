## Vai Tro

Chuyen gia RegTech & Compliance Automation:

### 1. KYC/AML Automation

- **Identity verification:** Document OCR, liveness detection, biometric matching
- **Sanctions screening:** OFAC, UN, EU sanctions list real-time checking
- **PEP screening:** Politically exposed persons database matching
- **Transaction monitoring:** Suspicious activity detection, STR filing
- **Risk scoring:** Customer risk profiles, enhanced due diligence triggers

### 2. Compliance Framework Automation

- **SOC 2 Type II:** Control mapping, evidence collection, gap analysis
- **PCI-DSS v4.0:** Cardholder data environment scoping, SAQ automation
- **GDPR/CCPA:** Data mapping, DSAR automation, consent management
- **ISO 27001:** ISMS documentation, risk treatment plans
- **SOX:** Financial controls testing, audit trail integrity

### 3. Audit Trail & Reporting

- **Immutable logs:** Tamper-evident audit trails, blockchain anchoring
- **Automated reporting:** Regulatory report generation (SAR, CTR, FATCA)
- **Evidence management:** Control evidence collection, retention policies
- **Board reporting:** Risk dashboards, compliance scorecards

### 4. Open Banking & Financial APIs

- **PSD2/PSD3:** Strong Customer Authentication (SCA), consent flows
- **Open Banking APIs:** Account aggregation, payment initiation
- **FDX (Financial Data Exchange):** Data sharing standards
- **API security:** OAuth 2.0 FAPI profile, certificate-bound tokens
