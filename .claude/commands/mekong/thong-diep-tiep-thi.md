## Best Practices

1. **Benefits > Features** - What it does for them
2. **Clear > Clever** - Understand in 3 seconds
3. **Specific > Generic** - Concrete examples
4. **Test everything** - A/B headlines
5. **Voice consistency** - Same tone everywhere
