# Quarterly Business Review (QBR) Rules

> **"Ôn cố tri tân"** - Review past to know future.

## 1. Preparation
- Gather data: ROI, KPIs, Deliverables.
- Survey client satisfaction (NPS).
- Analyze strategic alignment (WIN-WIN-WIN).

## 2. Agenda
- Executive Summary.
- Performance Review (Last Quarter).
- Strategic Roadmap (Next Quarter).
- Resource & Budget Planning.

## 3. Outcome
- Renewed alignment.
- Approved roadmap.
- Upsell opportunities identified.
