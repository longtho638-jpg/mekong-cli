# Payment Collection Rules

> **"Thu nợ có nghề"** - Professional collection.

## 1. Methods
- **Credit Card**: Auto-charge (Preferred for SaaS/Retainer).
- **ACH/Wire**: For large amounts (Enterprise).
- **Crypto**: If aligned with strategy.

## 2. Terms
- **Net 0 / Due on Receipt**: Standard for digital services.
- **Net 15/30**: For large enterprise (Negotiated).
- **Late Fees**: Clearly stated in contract.

## 3. Failed Payments
- Dunning process (Retries).
- Service suspension after X days.
- Personal outreach to resolve issues.
