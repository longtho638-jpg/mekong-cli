# Financial Reporting Rules

> **"Minh bạch tài chính"** - Financial transparency.

## 1. Frequency
- **Weekly**: Cash flow pulse.
- **Monthly**: P&L, Balance Sheet, Burn Rate.
- **Quarterly**: Forecast vs Actuals.

## 2. Metrics (KPIs)
- **MRR/ARR**: Recurring Revenue.
- **Churn Rate**: Revenue & Customer churn.
- **LTV:CAC**: Unit economics.
- **Gross Margin**: Efficiency.

## 3. Audience
- **Internal**: Founders, Dept Heads.
- **External**: Investors, Board.
