# Invoice Automation Rules

> **"Tiền về tự động"** - Automated cash in.

## 1. Tools
- **Stripe/QuickBooks/Xero**: Integrated billing.
- **Webhooks**: Trigger actions on payment events.

## 2. Workflow
- **Generation**: Auto-create invoice on schedule.
- **Sending**: Email to billing contact.
- **Reminders**: Auto-chase overdue payments (D+3, D+7, D+15).

## 3. Reconciliation
- Auto-match payments to invoices.
- Sync with accounting software.
- Dashboard for outstanding AR.
