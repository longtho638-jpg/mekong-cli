# Feature Expansion Rules

> **"Mở rộng lãnh thổ"** - Expanding territory.

## 1. Identification
- Analyze usage data for power users.
- Listen to "I wish I could..." requests.
- Monitor competitor features.

## 2. Proposal
- Define problem & solution.
- Estimate impact & effort.
- Price as add-on or tier upgrade.

## 3. Execution
- Rapid prototyping.
- Beta testing with key users.
- Full rollout with marketing.
