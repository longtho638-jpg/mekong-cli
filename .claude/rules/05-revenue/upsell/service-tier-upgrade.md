# Service Tier Upgrade Rules

> **"Thăng hạng"** - Level up.

## 1. Triggers
- Usage limits reached.
- Need for advanced features/support.
- Business growth requiring scaling.

## 2. Process
- Automated notifications (80%, 90%, 100% usage).
- Personalized outreach from Account Manager.
- Trial of higher tier features.

## 3. Incentives
- Annual discount.
- Exclusive access/training.
- Priority support.
