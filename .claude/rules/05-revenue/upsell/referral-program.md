# Referral Program Rules

> **"Tiếng lành đồn xa"** - Good news spreads.

## 1. Structure
- **Reward**: Cash, Credit, or Charity donation.
- **Double-sided**: Benefit for referrer and referee.

## 2. Timing
- Ask after a "Wow" moment (NPS 9/10).
- Ask during QBRs.

## 3. Enablement
- Provide templates/assets for sharing.
- Make introduction easy.
- Track and pay out promptly.
