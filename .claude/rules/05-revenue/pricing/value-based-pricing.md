# Value-Based Pricing Rules

> **"Giá trị > Giá cả"** - Value over Price.

## 1. Philosophy
- Price based on outcome, not hours.
- Align with client's revenue growth.

## 2. Models
- **Percentage of Revenue**: Share in upside.
- **Tiered Packages**: Basic, Pro, Enterprise.
- **Performance Bonuses**: Tied to specific KPIs.

## 3. Negotiation
- Focus on ROI calculation.
- Anchor with high value.
- Offer options (Good, Better, Best).
