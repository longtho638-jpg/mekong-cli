## 1. When to take Equity
- High growth potential (Unicorn/Soonicorn).
- Strategic alignment (Ecosystem fit).
- Cash constraint but high value.

## 2. Structure
- **Advisory Shares**: 0.5% - 2% (Standard vesting).
- **Service for Equity**: Discount cash for equity.
- **Venture Studio**: 15% - 30% Co-founder stake.

## 3. Protection
- Anti-dilution clauses.
- Information rights.
- Tag-along rights.
