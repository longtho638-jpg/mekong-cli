# Retainer Model Rules

> **"Dòng chảy ổn định"** - Stable cashflow.

## 1. Structure
- **Monthly Flat Fee**: Predictable cost/revenue.
- **Scope**: Defined deliverables or dedicated capacity.
- **Term**: Minimum 3, 6, or 12 months.

## 2. Benefits
- **Client**: Priority access, consistent execution.
- **Agency**: Cashflow stability, resource planning.

## 3. Management
- Track utilization (don't over/under service).
- Regular reporting to justify value.
- Rollover unused hours (optional/limited).
