# Churn Prevention Rules

> **"Giữ chân khách hàng"** - Keep them close.

## 1. Prediction
- Identify at-risk users (Low usage, support tickets).
- Engagement scoring.

## 2. Intervention
- Automated emails (Value reminders).
- In-app nudges.
- CSM outreach.

## 3. Involuntary Churn
- Dunning management (Failed card updates).
- Smart retries.
- Account updater services.
