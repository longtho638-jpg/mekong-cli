# KYC/AML Protocols Rules

> **"Biết khách hàng"** - Know Your Customer / Anti-Money Laundering.

## 1. Process
- **CIP (Customer Identification Program)**: Verify identity (ID, Selfie).
- **CDD (Customer Due Diligence)**: Assess risk.
- **EDD (Enhanced Due Diligence)**: For high-risk customers.

## 2. Monitoring
- Transaction monitoring for suspicious patterns.
- Sanctions screening (OFAC, PEPs).

## 3. Reporting
- SAR (Suspicious Activity Report).
- Record keeping (5 years).
