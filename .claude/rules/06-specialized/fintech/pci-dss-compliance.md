# PCI-DSS Compliance Rules

> **"An toàn thanh toán"** - Payment security.

## 1. Levels
- Determine Level (1-4) based on transaction volume.

## 2. Requirements
- Firewall configuration.
- Do not use vendor defaults.
- Protect cardholder data.
- Encrypt transmission.
- Anti-virus software.
- Restrict access (Need to know).

## 3. Implementation
- Use Tokenization (Stripe Elements).
- Avoid touching raw PAN (Primary Account Number).
- Regular vulnerability scans.
