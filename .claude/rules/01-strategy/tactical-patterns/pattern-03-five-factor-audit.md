# Tactical Pattern 03: The 5-Factor Audit

> **Ngũ Sự Analysis**

## Context
Use for Strategy Assessment (Chapter 1) or periodically.

## Pattern
1. **Đạo (Moral Law)**: Is the mission clear and shared?
2. **Thiên (Heaven)**: Is the market timing right? (Macro)
3. **Địa (Earth)**: Do we have a defensible position? (Micro/Moat)
4. **Tướng (Commander)**: Is the leadership capable?
5. **Pháp (Method)**: Are operations disciplined?

## Output
A scorecard (1-10) for each factor to identify the weakest link.
