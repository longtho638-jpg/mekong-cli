# Lead Generation Recipe

Automatically hunt for leads based on domain/keyword.

## Step 1: Define Target

Specify the domain or keyword to target.

Input: `domain` or `keyword`

## Step 2: Search & Scrape

Search for potential leads using web scraping.

Tools:

- Google search
- LinkedIn lookup
- Website scraping

## Step 3: Extract Contacts

Extract email and contact information.

Output:

- CEO/Founder email
- Company info
- Social profiles

## Step 4: Validate & Save

Validate email addresses and save to database.

Validation:

- Email format check
- Domain verification
- Dedup existing contacts
